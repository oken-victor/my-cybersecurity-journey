## Cyber Attacks
Cyber attackers are individuals or groups who attempt to exploit system
vulnerabilities for personal, financial, or strategic gain.

---

## Types of Attackers

### White Hat Hackers
- Work with permission
- Help organizations find and fix security weaknesses

### Gray Hat Hackers
- May work with or without permission
- Often have mixed or personal motives

### Black Hat Hackers
- Act illegally
- Exploit systems for malicious purposes

### Organized Hackers
- Often work with governments or large organizations
- Focus on intelligence gathering, disruption, or awareness creation

---

## Internal and External Threats

### Internal Threats
- Employees or insiders
- Can accidentally or intentionally mishandle confidential data
- May lead to data leaks or external attacks

### External Threats
- Attackers outside the organization
- Include hackers, cybercriminals, and nation-state actors

---

## Key Note
Understanding different attacker types and threat sources helps
organizations design better security defenses.
