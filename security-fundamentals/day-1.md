## What Is Cybersecurity?
Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting individuals, organizations, and governments from digital attacks by securing data, systems, and devices against unauthorized access or harm.

## Levels of Cybersecurity

### Personal Security
- Protecting personal data and identity
- Using strong passwords
- Keeping devices updated
- Protecting IP and personal information

### Organizational Security
- Everyone is responsible for protecting company data
- Focus on securing customer and business information

### Government Security
- Protecting national data and infrastructure
- Ensuring safety and national stability against cyber threats

## Key Takeaway
Cybersecurity affects individuals, organizations, and governments, and everyone has a role to play.
