## Protection of Information in Each State

### Processing
Processing refers to data that is actively being used to perform operations within a system.

### Storage
Storage refers to data that is saved in memory, databases, disks, or other storage devices.

### Transmission
Transmission refers to data that is traveling between information systems, such as over networks or the internet.

Protecting data in all three states is essential for effective cybersecurity.

---

## Security Measures
The following measures are used to protect information systems:

- Awareness, training, and education
- Technology controls
- Policies and procedures

These measures work together to reduce security risks.

---

## Consequences of Security Breaches
Security breaches can have serious impacts, including:

- Reputational damage
- Vandalism
- Theft
- Loss of revenue
- Damage to intellectual property

---

## Key Note
Security breaches can have devastating consequences for an organization.  
Therefore, it is crucial to take appropriate steps and implement strong security measures to protect against cyber attacks.
